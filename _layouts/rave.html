Overview

My project functioned as a flashing distorted mirror that people would dance in front of to the rave music. The project used people’s sight and the feedback that they got from my project was to see themselves “raving” in a more exciting and atmospheric way.

Interaction

The digital component of my project is a pixelated dot image that randomly changes dot color. It “pointilizes” the image that is taken in from the web cam. The physical component is the web cam part of the digital component. During the Rave the web cam was pointed at the door so that each guest that came into the room was immediately greeted with a giant screen that showed their image with dots.
